About UK Visa Calculator

Our Purpose

UK Visa Calculator is a free tool designed to help international applicants estimate the total cost of UK visa and immigration applications. We provide comprehensive cost breakdowns including application fees, Immigration Health Surcharge, and optional priority services.

What We Offer

  • Accurate cost calculations for all major UK visa routes
  • Coverage of work, study, family, visitor, and settlement visas
  • Support for dependants and priority processing options
  • Regularly updated fee information
  • Clear, easy-to-understand cost breakdowns

⚠️ Important Notice

UK Visa Calculator is not affiliated with the UK government or any official immigration authority.

All cost estimates provided are for planning purposes only. UK visa fees and Immigration Health Surcharge rates are subject to change without notice. You must verify all costs with official UK government sources before submitting your application.

Official source: gov.uk/visa-fees

Data Sources

Our fee information is based on publicly available data from UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI). We strive to keep our data current, but official government sources should always be consulted for the most up-to-date information.
